About PhotoFinder

Our mission is to help you rediscover your photo memories by making them easily searchable by the people in them.

Our Story

PhotoFinder was created out of a simple frustration: finding photos of specific people in our ever-growing digital photo collections.

We realized that while services like Google Photos offer basic face recognition, they often lack the fine-grained control that many users need. That's why we built PhotoFinder - to give you powerful tools to filter and find your photos by the faces that matter to you.

Our app securely connects to your Google Photos account, scans your images for faces, and creates an intuitive interface for you to filter and find exactly what you're looking for.

How It Works

Our Technology

Face Recognition AI

We use advanced machine learning algorithms to accurately detect and recognize faces in your photos.

Secure Integration

Our Google Photos integration uses OAuth for secure access without ever storing your Google password.

Django Backend

Built on the powerful Django framework for reliability, scalability, and security.
